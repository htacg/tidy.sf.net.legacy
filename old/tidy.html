HTML Tidy Library Project

Dave Raggett's HTML Tidy is going open source!

A quorum of developers have pitched in on a Source Forge project. We two primary goals. First, we need a home where all the patches and fixes that the community has contributed to be collected and incorporated into the product. Second, a library form of Tidy is needed to make it easier to incorporate Tidy into other software.
